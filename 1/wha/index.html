The above places people together in clusters. This is intended to represent a physical location. It is hoped that the cross-disciplinary interaction that can take place when people from diverse backgrounds, skills and experiences are placed together can help foster the development of unique solutions. In addition, care is taken to ensure that a new idea can find its way to fruition, as there are those who can design, build and package and then communicate this to others.

The section below looks at the "what". That is, any of the above people or groups may need any of that which is listed below to get their job done. They may need office supplies, equipment, a place to meet (a building) and generate products or services that others can use. They may generate waste that needs to be recovered or information that needs to be disseminated. They may need to follow standards or develop a unique process that helps the assembly of equipment to work that much better.

The section above deals with the noun in a sentence (and the location of that noun), the section below deals with the verb and the object in the sentence as well as the recipient; who receives the benefit from the work done.
